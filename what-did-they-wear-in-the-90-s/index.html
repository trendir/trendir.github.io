What Did They Wear In The 90 S A Complete Guide To The Fashion Of The Decade

Written by Christine Aug 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When you think of the 90's, you may think of iconic TV shows like Friends, hit boy bands like Backstreet Boys, or classic movies like Clueless. But what did they wear in the 90's? The fashion of the decade was just as memorable, with trends like grunge, hip hop, and preppy styles dominating the scene. In this guide, we'll take a deep dive into the fashion of the 90's and answer all your burning questions about what people wore during this iconic decade.

The 90's was a decade of experimentation and individuality when it comes to fashion. People were no longer confined to strict dress codes, and personal style was celebrated. However, with so many different trends emerging, it can be overwhelming to keep up with what was popular and what wasn't. If you're unsure about what to wear to a 90's-themed party or just want to learn more about the fashion of the decade, keep reading.

So, what did they wear in the 90's? One of the most iconic fashion movements of the decade was grunge. This style was characterized by ripped jeans, oversized flannel shirts, and combat boots. Another big trend was hip hop fashion, which included baggy pants, athletic wear, and chunky sneakers. Preppy fashion was also popular, with polo shirts, khakis, and loafers making a comeback. Other notable trends of the decade included crop tops, slip dresses, and chokers.

In summary, the fashion of the 90's was diverse and eclectic, with a range of different styles and trends emerging throughout the decade. From grunge to preppy to hip hop fashion, people were free to express themselves and experiment with their personal style.

Grungy Style: The Ultimate 90's Look

As mentioned earlier, grunge fashion was one of the most popular styles of the 90's. This look was inspired by the punk movement of the 80's and was characterized by a rough, unkempt appearance. To achieve the ultimate grungy look, start with a pair of ripped jeans or overalls. Layer an oversized flannel shirt on top, and add a vintage band t-shirt underneath. Finish the look with a pair of combat boots, and you're good to go!

Hip Hop Fashion: Baggy is Better

If you were into hip hop in the 90's, chances are you rocked the baggy pants and oversized shirts look. To recreate this iconic style, start with a pair of loose-fitting pants, like joggers or cargo pants. Pair them with a baggy t-shirt or hoodie, and add a pair of chunky sneakers to complete the look. Don't be afraid to mix and match different colors and patterns to create a truly unique outfit.

Preppy Fashion: Timeless and Classic

Preppy fashion may have originated in the 80's, but it made a comeback in the 90's. This look was all about clean lines, neutral colors, and classic pieces like polo shirts and khakis. To create a preppy outfit, start with a collared shirt or sweater in a neutral color, like navy or beige. Add a pair of khakis or chinos, and finish the look with a pair of loafers or boat shoes. This look is perfect for a day at the office or a casual weekend brunch.

How to Accessorize in the 90's

No 90's outfit was complete without the right accessories. Chokers were a popular trend, as were statement earrings and layered necklaces. Baseball caps and bucket hats were also a staple of 90's fashion, as were backpacks and fanny packs. Don't be afraid to mix and match different accessories to create a unique look that's all your own.

The Iconic Slip Dress

The slip dress was one of the most iconic fashion trends of the 90's. This simple, yet elegant dress was made famous by celebrities like Kate Moss and Courtney Love. To recreate this look, start with a simple silk or satin slip dress in a neutral color, like black or white. Layer it over a t-shirt for a more casual look, or wear it on its own for a night out on the town.

Question and Answer

Q: What were some popular shoe styles in the 90's?

A: Some popular shoe styles in the 90's included chunky sneakers, combat boots, platform sandals, and Birkenstocks.

Q: Were crop tops really that popular in the 90's?

A: Yes, crop tops were a staple of 90's fashion, and were often paired with high-waisted jeans or skirts.

Q: What were some popular hairstyles in the 90's?

A: Some popular hairstyles in the 90's included the Rachel cut (as made famous by Jennifer Aniston in Friends), crimped hair, and the high ponytail.

Q: What were some popular denim trends in the 90's?

A: Acid wash jeans, high-waisted jeans, and denim jackets were all popular denim trends in the 90's.

Conclusion of What Did They Wear in the 90's

The fashion of the 90's was all about individuality and self-expression. With a range of different styles and trends emerging throughout the decade, people were free to experiment with their personal style and create unique looks that reflected their personality. From grunge to preppy to hip hop fashion, the fashion of the 90's continues to inspire us today.
